### High Priority

#### Daily reminder alert

- Set a reminder notification to alert users daily about their tasks or goals.
- The notification should remind users how many goals are remaining for the day and encourage them to complete their tasks.
- Initially, the reminder alert will be set to trigger at a specific time each day, but users should have the option to customize the time according to their preferences.
- The reminder alert should be designed to be non-intrusive, allowing users to dismiss it easily if they are unable to complete their tasks at that moment.

#### Targeted reminder alert

- Implement a targeted reminder alert that focuses on specific goals or tasks that users have set for themselves.
- The alert should provide users with a summary of their progress towards those specific goals and offer encouragement to keep them motivated.
- Users should have the ability to customize the content of the targeted reminder alert, allowing them to include specific messages or motivational quotes that resonate with their goals.
- The targeted reminder alert should be designed to be visually appealing and engaging, using colors and graphics that align with the overall aesthetic of the Goal Tracker app.
- The alert timing should be timed to coincide with moments when each goal has been completed before, or perhaps slightly earlier to provide a nudge to complete the goal. This timing can be adjusted based on user preferences and behavior patterns.
